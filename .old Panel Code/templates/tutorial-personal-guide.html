Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Pending List se kaam start kare: Pending List me sabhi incoming records hote hain. Yahi se correction/new add dono hota hai.
  2. Photo update process: Photo column me photo ke niche Edit option khole, fir Upload Photo par click karke image replace ya new upload kare.
  3. Verify stage: Har required field check kare aur Verify button se record ko Verified List me move kare. Bulk verify ke liye checkbox ka use kare.
  4. Final approve stage: Verified List me final review ke baad Approve kare. Approve hone ke baad record final flow me lock ho jata hai.
  5. Important lock rule: Approved data me direct correction normally allowed nahi hota, isliye approve se pehle class/section/name/photo sab re-check kare.
  6. Pool List recovery: Agar correction ke liye record wapas lana ho, Pool List me select karke Retrieve par click kare. Record Pending List me shift ho jayega.

Daily Best Practice

  • Day start me Pending backlog clear kare.
  • Verify aur Approve ke beech final visual check compulsory rakhe.
  • Export/download se pehle filter (class/section) double-check kare.
  • Ek login ko multiple operators me share na kare.
  • Sub-staff ko minimum required permissions hi de.
